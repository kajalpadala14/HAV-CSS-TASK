American Woman Found Emaciated and ‘Left to Die’ Chained to Tree in India: A 50-year-old American woman was discovered chained to a tree in India. Her cries were heard by a shepherd who alerted authorities. She reported that her husband had tied her there and “left her to die.” The incident has raised concerns about domestic violence and the safety of women .

‘Flying Rivers’ Causing Devastating Floods in India: Heavy monsoon rains have led to floods in several parts of India. These floods are caused by what scientists call “flying rivers,” which are moisture-laden winds that transport water vapor across the Indian subcontinent. The resulting floods have affected communities, causing casualties and displacements .

Hopes for Survivors Fade in Deadly India Landslides: In Kerala, massive landslides triggered by heavy rains have resulted in the loss of 182 lives, with nearly 200 people still missing. Rescue operations continue in the affected areas, but hopes of finding survivors are diminishing .
